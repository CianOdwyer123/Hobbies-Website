The editing process I do:

The second step to making a YouTube video is the editing process. This is the most time consuming process of the video but also the important. Without any editing, your videos will look terrible.
The first part of editing is the cutting. This is where you find parts of silence, parts you messed up on or just parts you donâ€™t want in your video. This will shorten your video length but will improve the look of it immensely.
Next is the background music, sounds or images you want to insert and position in the video. The cuts you made previously will really help with changing the volumes of the music and the sound from your camera.
Now the last stage of editing a video is the transitions and animations. This will keep your video moving and make it different from others.
There you go. Now you are ready to upload it to YouTube The editing software I use is called "Final Cut Pro X". It was made by Apple.
